how easy is to migrate to mexico

The most common and easiest way to move to Mexico is to apply for a residence permit under “economic solvency”; in other words, if you can prove you have enough money. Additionally, you can move for work and retirement, depending on which option suits you best.

what is the cost of living in mexico

The cost of living in Mexico is generally lower than in the United States, but varies depending on location and lifestyle. In 2024, a single person in Mexico can expect to live on about $982 per month, while a family of four can expect to live on about $2,375 per month.
