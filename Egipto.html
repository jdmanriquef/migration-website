EGYPT

How is migration in egypt

Egypt hosts a significant number of migrants and refugees, especially from:

Sudan, Syria, Ethiopia, Yemen, and other conflict-affected countries.

Some use Egypt as a transit country on the way to Europe.

Egypt has limited application of the 1951 Refugee Convention, so refugee rights can be restricted, and many live in tough conditions.

Current issues and challenges:

Urban pressure: Cities are growing fast and often without proper planning.

Youth unemployment: A major reason people migrate is the lack of jobs or low salaries.

Refugee management: Egypt works with UNHCR, but protection and services for refugees are sometimes limited.
