how is migration in italy

Italy's migration patterns include a mix of long-term immigrants, asylum seekers, and humanitarian migrants. In 2023, Italy saw a large influx of migrants, including many unaccompanied minors.

what is the cost of living in italy

The cost of living in Italy varies by city, lifestyle, and whether you rent or own a home. On average, a single person spends around €1,400–€1,600 per month, while a family of four spends around €2,750.
